Choy Lee Fut 5th Generation Master Chu Siu Kei

Master Chu Shiu Kei (朱兆基師傅), originally from Qingyuan, Guangdong (廣東清遠), is a fifth-generation inheritor of Choy Lee Fut (蔡李佛). As early as 1963, he began his martial arts training under Grandmaster To Hon Cheung (杜漢璋宗師). He developed deep understanding in a wide range of martial disciplines, including the Eighteen Martial Skills (十八般武藝), Buddha Palm (佛家掌), Wooden Dummy Techniques (木人樁法), and internal energy training such as the Hun Yuen Qigong (渾元氣功) for health and vitality.

In 1968, he established his own school and began accepting students. Today, his disciples are spread all around the world.

During the 1970s, Master Chu published a series of books on Choy Lee Fut Kung Fu, which included topics such as Wooden Dummy Techniques (木人樁法), Dragon Form (龍形拳), Tiger Form (虎形拳), and Snake Form (蛇形拳).
