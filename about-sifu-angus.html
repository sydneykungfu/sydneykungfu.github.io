Sifu Angus

Sifu Angus Tse, the head coach of Tse Kung Fu Academy. He borned in the Kung Fu hub, Hong Kong. He has over 20 years of experience in Choy Lee Fut Kung Fu. Sifu Angus is authentic lineage in
Hung Sing Choy Lee Fut Fong Yuk Shu lineage (雄勝蔡李佛 - 方玉書系)

Demostrated on Grand Master Chu Book

Sifu Angus, demostrated Ng Lun Ma on Grand Master Chu's book "Choy Lay Fut Kung Fu" (蔡李佛功夫) published in 2009.

Ng Lun Ma (2009)

Qualifications

  • 6th DAN holder certified by the Hong Kong Chin Woo Authentic Association.
  • Instructor in Choy Lee Fut class in Hong Kong Ching Woo.
  • Choy Lee Fut 6th Generation, direct disciple of Grand Master Chu Siu Ki.
  • Working With Children Check (WWCC) clearance.
  • Senior First Aid accredited.

Martial Arts Background

Sifu Angus began his martial arts journey with Choy Lee Fut Kung Fu and later expanded his expertise by training in various styles, including Choi Mok with Sifu Lau Biu (劉標), Chow Gar Southern Praying Mantis with Sifu Hang Ng (吳煥亨), Muay Thai with Samkor Kiatmontep, and Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu.
