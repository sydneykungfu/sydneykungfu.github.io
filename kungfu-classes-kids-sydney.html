5-Level Progression System

Our structured level system gives children clear goals and a sense of achievement as they advance through their Kung Fu journey.

🐰

Level 1 — Rabbit

First steps into Kung Fu. Children learn basic stances, simple strikes, and class routines. Builds listening skills and gets comfortable moving with the group.

🐍

Level 2 — Snake

Developing body control and flexibility. Children can perform foundational forms, follow combinations, and begin to understand timing and distance.

🐯

Level 3 — Tiger

Growing in power and confidence. Children execute techniques with strength, practise pad work, and can apply basic self-defence under guidance.

🦢

Level 4 — Crane

Precision and composure. Children demonstrate balanced footwork, focused technique, and can perform longer forms with correct posture and flow.

🐉

Level 5 — Dragon

The highest level. Children show leadership, help younger students, and perform advanced forms and self-defence with maturity and skill.

Frequently Asked Questions

What age is this class for?

Our kids Kung Fu classes are designed for children aged 6 to 14. Younger children (3–5) can join our Preschool Kung Fu program.

Does my child need any experience?

No prior experience is needed. Our 5-level system starts at the very beginning and progresses at each child's pace.

What should my child wear?

Comfortable clothing they can move in — sports clothes and trainers are fine. No special uniform is needed to start.

Can I watch the class?

Yes, parents are welcome to stay and watch. Our outdoor training locations make it easy to observe your child's progress.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! We offer a free trial class so your child can experience Kung Fu before committing. Book your free trial here.
