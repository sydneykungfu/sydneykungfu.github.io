Frequently Asked Questions

I'm in my 40s/50s - am I too old to start Kung Fu?

Not at all! Our adult classes are designed for all fitness levels and ages. We modify techniques to accommodate different abilities and focus on building strength and flexibility gradually. Many of our students start in their 40s, 50s, and beyond.

What should I expect in my first adult Kung Fu class?

Your first class will include a warm-up, basic stance work, and simple hand techniques. We focus on proper form and building a solid foundation. No prior experience is needed - we'll guide you through everything step by step.

How often should I attend classes as an adult beginner?

We recommend attending once per week to start, then gradually increasing to twice weekly as your fitness improves. Our flexible schedule allows you to choose the class times that work best with your lifestyle.
