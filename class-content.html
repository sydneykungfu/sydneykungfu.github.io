Forms are not the objective; they are vehicles. There are a lot of san sau, drills, and pad works in between. Explore the syllabus to understand the progression and techniques taught at Tse Kung Fu Academy.

Classes structure will includes:

  • Tranditional Choy Lee Fut Forms.
  • Application Drills, San Sau - Understand the usage. Self-defence.
  • Pad Works - Accuracy and Timing.
  • Conditioning - Strength and Flexibility.
  • Light Sparring - Intermediate Level or above.

Our Kung Fu classes in Sydney are designed for all levels, from beginners to advanced practitioners. We embrased small class, hand by hand training, and personal attention to ensure that each student receives the guidance they need to progress at their own pace. We focus on practical self-defense techniques, traditional forms, and physical conditioning.

We offer an optional ranking / sash system for students who wish to progress in their Kung Fu journey.
