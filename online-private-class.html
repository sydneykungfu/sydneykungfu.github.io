Online Private Kung Fu Class

Each online private class is 60 minutes long and tailored to your specific needs. Whether you're a beginner or an advanced student, the session will focus on your goals and areas of improvement.

The class content is based on our syllabus, which includes forms, drills, and techniques from Choy Lee Fut Kung Fu.

Fee is about $63 AUD per session. Payments are secured by Shopify and Stripe. Check the fee in here.

The video call is powered by Google Meet, ensuring a seamless and high-quality virtual training experience.

Note: You will receive a Google Meet link via email after booking your session. Please ensure you have a stable internet connection and a quiet space to train.
For the best experience, we recommend using a computer or tablet with a camera and microphone.
Headphones are optional but can help reduce background noise.
