Online Private Class Details

Each online private class is 60 minutes long and tailored to your specific needs. Whether you're a beginner or an advanced student, the session will focus on your goals and areas of improvement.

The class content is based on our syllabus, which includes forms, drills, and techniques from Choy Lee Fut Kung Fu.

The video call is powered by Google Meet, ensuring a seamless and high-quality virtual training experience.

How It Works

  1. Book Your Time Slot: Use our Calendly link to schedule your session.
  2. Secure Your Payment: Proceed to our HardClutch Shopify store to complete your payment. Payments are secured by Shopify and Stripe.
  3. Note: Tse Kung Fu Academy is is subsidiary of HardClutch. All payments are processed through HardClutch.
  4. Join the Class: A Google Meet link will be sent to your email after booking. Join the session at your scheduled time.
