About Sifu Angus

Sifu Angus is a lifelong martial artist with over 20 years of experience in Choy Lee Fut Kung Fu, trained in Hong Kong under Grand Master Chu Shiu Ki. As a direct disciple and 6th DAN holder certified by the Hong Kong Chin Woo Authentic Association, he represents a prestigious martial lineage.

Beyond Choy Lee Fut, he has trained in Muay Thai, Boxing, BJJ, Choi Mok, and Chow Gar Southern Praying Mantis, giving him a broad, well-rounded approach to martial arts.
