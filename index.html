About Our Choy Lee Fut Kung Fu

Our Choy Lay Fut lineage is the Hung Sing Choy Lay Fut, Fong Yuk Shu lineage. This is one of the most popular Southern Chinese martial arts, focusing on strength, flexibility, discipline, and philosophy. Learn authentic techniques passed down through generations in a friendly, supportive environment.

About Sifu Angus

Angus practiced Choy Lee Fut in Hong Kong for 20 years and is a direct disciple of Grand Master Chu Shiu Ki. Angus holds a 6th DAN from the Hong Kong Chin Woo Authentic Association, the oldest Kung Fu association. He has also practiced various styles, such as Muay Thai, Boxing, BJJ, Choi Mok, and Chow Gar Southern Praying Mantis. As an engineer, Angus explains martial arts in a modern, scientific way.
