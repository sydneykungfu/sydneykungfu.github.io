Choy Lee Fut Kung Fu

Choy Lee Fut (蔡李佛) is a powerful traditional Kung Fu. It blends powerful hand techniques, dynamic footwork, and internal development. Founded in 1836 by Chan Heung, it honors three key influences—Choy Fook, Lee Yau San, and Chan Heung himself—combining their names to form one of the most comprehensive and respected systems in Chinese martial arts. Choy Lee Fut offers a holistic path that trains both body and mind. It is ideal for students of all ages looking to build strength, confidence, and self-mastery through a deep, cultural tradition.

About Sifu Angus

Sifu Angus is a lifelong martial artist with over 20 years of experience in Choy Lee Fut Kung Fu, having trained in Hong Kong under Grand Master Chu Shiu Ki. He is a direct disciple and lineage holder, and currently holds a 6th DAN certification from the Hong Kong Chin Woo Authentic Association — the oldest and most prestigious Kung Fu organization in Hong Kong. In addition to Choy Lee Fut, Angus has cross-trained in multiple systems including Muay Thai, Western Boxing, Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (BJJ), Choi Mok, and Chow Gar Southern Praying Mantis. His diverse background gives him a well-rounded perspective on both traditional and modern fighting systems. With a professional background in engineering, Sifu Angus is known for teaching martial arts through a modern, analytical lens, breaking down techniques with biomechanical precision and practical logic — without losing the spirit of tradition.
